### 🕵️ CrediAI — Bilingual Fake News Detection
> Transformer-based deep learning system for English & Tamil fake news classification

| Model | Task | Accuracy |
|-------|------|----------|
| RoBERTa-base | English Articles | **93.2%** |
| DistilRoBERTa | English Headlines | **91.5%** |
| XLM-RoBERTa | Tamil Content | **89.7%** |

- 🏆 **1st Prize** at TECHTRON'26, PPG College of Arts and Science
- 🖥️ Deployed as an interactive **Streamlit** app
